Pricing & Comparison

Is Uber or Bolt cheaper than a taxi in Lisbon?

Often, Uber or Bolt can be slightly cheaper for standard rides. However, during peak hours, high demand, or late at night, they use 'surge pricing', which can make the ride significantly more expensive. For peace of mind, our private transfer service offers a guaranteed fixed price, so you know the exact cost upfront, with no surprises.

How much does a taxi from Lisbon airport to city centre cost?

A regular taxi from Lisbon airport to the city centre (e.g., Baixa, Chiado) typically costs between €15 and €25. This price can increase with traffic, time of day, and extra luggage fees. A pre-booked private transfer avoids this uncertainty with a clear, all-inclusive price.

Do you need cash for Uber or taxis in Portugal?

For Uber/Bolt, your card on the app is used. For taxis, while many now accept credit cards, it's not guaranteed. Some drivers may prefer cash or their machine might "be broken". With our service, you have the option to arrange payment in advance, so you don't need to worry about cash on arrival.

Airport Logistics

Are Ubers allowed at Lisbon airport?

Yes, but the pick-up location is in a specific, often crowded, car park outside the arrivals terminal, which can be confusing for first-time visitors. Our drivers provide a personal meet & greet service right at the arrivals gate. They will be holding a sign with your name, eliminating all stress and confusion.

What is the best way to travel from Lisbon airport to the city?

It depends on your priority. The Metro is cheapest, but can be difficult with luggage. Taxis and Ubers are convenient but have variable pricing and potential queues. A private transfer is the best option for comfort, convenience, and a stress-free start to your trip, especially for families or those with luggage.

Do you tip taxi drivers in Lisbon?

Tipping is not obligatory in Portugal, but it's appreciated. A small tip of 5-10% or rounding up the fare is a nice gesture for good service. With our fixed-price service, you are not expected to tip, but if you feel the service was exceptional, your driver will be grateful.
